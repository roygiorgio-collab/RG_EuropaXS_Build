Europa XS Links & Resources

A curated collection of helpful links for builders, pilots, and enthusiasts of the Europa XS.

Official & Club Sites

  • Europa Aircraft – Official Manufacturer Website
    The official source for specifications, kit options, and factory support.
  • The Europa Club – Builder and Pilot Association
    Comprehensive support for builders: manuals, events, and modification resources.
  • EuropaOwners.org
    Community-run resource with photos, maintenance tips, and builder logs.

Builder Blogs & Personal Sites

  • Get Your Wings – Europa XS Build Series
    An engaging restoration story of a Europa XS brought back to life.
  • Build Your Own Airplane Blog – Europa XS
    Step-by-step coverage of the kit assembly process and lessons learned.
  • MyKitLog.com
    A massive archive of Europa builder logs from around the world.
  • Ray Hillenbrand’s Europa XS Project
    A personal record of construction milestones and customization ideas.
  • Europa Owners Gallery
    Dozens of beautifully finished Europa XS aircraft for inspiration.

Social Media & Forums

  • Facebook: Europa Aircraft Owners Group
    The most active online hub for Europa builders and pilots.
  • Facebook: Europa XS Enthusiasts
    Focused discussions on flight performance, handling, and maintenance.
  • Reddit: r/HomebuiltAircraft
    A welcoming community for all experimental aircraft builders.
  • FlightClub.io Forum
    Discussions on avionics, composites, and build techniques.

Parts, Support & Reference

    Regulations and technical guidance for amateur-built aircraft.
