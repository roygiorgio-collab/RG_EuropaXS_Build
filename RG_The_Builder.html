About the Builder

I’ve always had a passion for aviation. My wife, Karen, has been wonderfully patient with this project—especially her tolerance of the noises, smells, and dust coming from the garage. Her encouragement (and creativity in finding ways to fund my early flight lessons) made this dream possible.

This page shares the story behind my Europa build—where the inspiration came from, how the airplane was chosen, and a bit about my background. I hope it helps future builders understand that this is as much a journey of patience as it is of craftsmanship.
